- Subject and Activity

subject - The ID of the test subject

activity - The type of activity performed when the corresponding measurements were taken

- Metrics:

1- Time/Frequency: Whether the time or the frequency of an activity were measured. 

2-Accelerometer/Gyroscope: Whether the activity were measured by the Accelerometer or the Gyroscope of the Device.

3- Body/Gravity: Wether the record is related to Body movement or to a Gravity effect.

4- Mean/Std: Wether the metric is a Mean or a Sandard Deviation of the measured data.
